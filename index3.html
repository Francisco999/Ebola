How is Ebola effecting people around the world?

In aproximately 7 months, about 5,689 people died from Ebola in six countries; Liberia, Guinea, Sierra Leone, Nigeria, United States, and Mali. There are 3,222 death in Liberia 1,899 in Sierra Leone, 1,462 in Guinea,8 in Nigeria. You can get Ebola at any age; a two-year-old toddler died in December 2013 in Meliandou, a small village in south-eastern Guinea. There were more than 18,000 total deaths in the six countries.
