Where did Ebola originate from?

Ebola orginiated in Sudan and Zaire, in 1976. A few months later, the second Ebola virus spreaded to Yambuku, Zaire. Ebola was named after the Ebola River in Zaire, where it was first discovered. Ebola also effected monkeys.
